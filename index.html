ABOUT MEN OF HONOR CONVENTION

The MOH therefore provides the men of this local Assembly with the platform to lead. In leading, to prepare ourselves for that task by confronting the matters that affects us; our manhood, our fatherhood, our brotherhood, our business and money, our career, our role in politics and government and other social, cultural and economic environment where we live and where we are to take dominion. This vision is also consistent with the vision of this local assembly that we often recite “building leaders, changing the world”. Programmes are being lined up with speakers both from among us and from outside who will share important experiences of both challenges and success. It also involves practical activities such as providing support for the less privileged in our immediate community. It is my honour therefore to call us all to commit ourselves to this walk with God as we embark on the various programmes and activities that would help us fulfill our God-ordained leadership position. This vision is for men only.

MEN OF HONOUR was inaugurated with the maiden assembly on Saturday 09 October, 2010. The vision is a call to all the men to take their place as leaders in every spheres of life. That God-ordained leadership is now more compelling than ever before; at home and family, at the workplace and the marketplace, in the local assembly, in the community and in the nation as a whole.

At the inaugural meeting, Pastor shared the vision of MOH and set the tone for a fellowship of men of GLA, Abuja. However, Pastor lamented man’s inability to live up to God’s expectations because he has become frustrated by the worldly pressures and pleasures, he faces every day. He recounted how he sees men in church who genuinely love God but are distracted by the world around them. Men terrorized by the enemy or unable to discover their identity as leaders who fulfill their God-ordained purpose.
